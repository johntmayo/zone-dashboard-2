About the Zone Dashboard

Altagether Zone Dashboard — trust, orientation, and grounding

← Open Dashboard

Welcome to the Zone Dashboard!

This dashboard is here to help you track what's happening in your zone. You can edit most fields, add missing addresses, and enter information about properties and neighbors. We also include contact information and, when available, public data such as damage status and recent sales. The dashboard is meant to be actively updated by you, so the more you add, the more useful it becomes.

Contact Status

The dashboard does not automatically know whether you have contacted a household. One of its main purposes is to help track which households you've reached and which you have not. Please log your outreach so the dashboard reflects the real status of contact in your zone.

Data Accuracy

The dashboard pulls from a mix of sources, including voter rolls and LA County damage data. We've worked to make it as accurate as possible, but it will not be perfect. There may be missing addresses, missing people, or outdated information. You are the source of truth for what is happening in your zone. If something is missing, you can add it using the Add Records feature under the Neighbors tab.

Privacy and Data Use

Only you and Altagether leadership have access to the data in this dashboard. We will never share your data with any individual or organization without your explicit permission.

An Evolving Tool

We are continuing to improve the dashboard by adding features and refining what is already here. If you have ideas, notice something confusing, or run into a problem, we'd love to hear from you. You can use the link in the bottom-left corner of the screen to send us a message, or contact John directly at john@altagether.org.
